\begin{block}{Why Not Just Use a Camera?}

\begin{columns}[T]

    \begin{column}{0.58\linewidth}
        Vision-based person identification struggles under real-world conditions:
        \vspace{0.2cm}
        \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{Low illumination}: degraded visual features
        \item \textbf{Adverse weather}: fog and rain reduce reliability
        \item \textbf{Occlusion}: missing body information
        \item \textbf{Privacy}: facial sensing raises ethical concerns
        \end{itemize}

        mmWave radar provides a privacy-preserving alternative,
        maintaining robustness without capturing identifiable imagery.
    \end{column}
